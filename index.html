Frequently Asked Questions

How does RabtaCar protect my privacy?

Your phone number is NEVER displayed on the tag or shared with anyone. When someone scans your tag, they send a message through our system, and we forward the alert to you via SMS. Complete anonymity guaranteed.

Do I need to download an app?

No! RabtaCar works through WhatsApp, which most people already have. The person alerting you just scans the QR code with their camera app - no special app needed by anyone.

Where should I place the tag?

Stick it on the INSIDE of your rear windshield, bottom corner. This protects it from weather and vandalism while keeping it easily visible and scannable.

How long does the tag last?

2-3 years! Our tags use UV-resistant lamination and premium vinyl, tested in Pakistani weather conditions (40-50¬∞C summers). Apply it once and forget about it.

Yes! We deliver ALL across Pakistan via TCS/Leopards courier. Major cities (Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad) get delivery in 1-2 days. Other cities in 2-4 days.

Can I use it for my bike/motorcycle?

Absolutely! RabtaCar works for cars, bikes, scooters, rickshaws, and any vehicle with a windshield or visible surface. Same price, same protection.
